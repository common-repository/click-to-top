== Description ==

<h4>Click to top is nice choice for go top in website.</h4>
Click to Top is a WordPress plugin that allows users to easily and quickly navigate to the top of a web page with just one click. This plugin adds a small button on the bottom-right corner of the website that will take users back to the top of the page when clicked. The button is customizable, and users can choose from different icon styles, sizes, colors, and animations to match their website's design.

The Click to Top plugin is incredibly user-friendly and can be easily installed and activated without any technical expertise. It is compatible with most WordPress themes and is optimized for performance to ensure that it doesn't slow down your website's loading time.

This plugin is particularly useful for websites that have long pages or articles, as it provides an easy way for users to navigate back to the top without having to scroll all the way up manually. It improves user experience, reduces bounce rates, and can even contribute to better search engine rankings.

== Frequently Asked Questions  ==

= What is Click to Top?  =

Click to Top is a WordPress plugin that adds a small button to the bottom-right corner of a website, allowing users to easily navigate to the top of the page with just one click. It enhances the user experience by providing a convenient and efficient way to navigate long pages or articles, reducing bounce rates and contributing to better search engine rankings. The Click to Top button is customizable, and users can choose from different icon styles, sizes, colors, and animations to match their website's design.

= Is Click to Top compatible with all WordPress themes? =

lick to Top is designed to be compatible with most WordPress themes

= Can I customize the appearance of the Click to Top button? =

Yes, Click to Top allows you to customize the appearance of the button to match your website's design. The plugin offers a range of customization options, such as changing the button size, color, shape, and animation. You can also choose from various icon styles, including FontAwesome and Dashicons, or upload your own custom icon. Additionally, Click to Top supports the use of custom CSS for further styling options. By default, the plugin adds a small arrow icon to the button, but you can easily change this to a different icon or text label if you prefer. These customization options make it easy to create a Click to Top button that is both functional and visually appealing for your website's users.

= Will Click to Top slow down my website's loading time? =

No, Click to Top is designed to be lightweight and optimized for performance to ensure that it does not slow down your website's loading time. The plugin uses efficient code and caching techniques to minimize any impact on your website's speed. In fact, Click to Top can even improve user experience and reduce bounce rates by providing a convenient way for users to navigate long pages or articles. However, it is always recommended to use a caching plugin and perform regular speed tests to ensure that your website is loading quickly and efficiently.
